PlayQuizEarn

App Description

PlayQuizEarn is a mobile application that allows users to earn rewards by engaging in various activities. The key features and earning methods in the app include:

Watch Videos to Earn Coins

  • Users can watch promotional videos within the app.
  • Each video watched rewards the user with a certain number of coins.
  • Coins can accumulate over time and be redeemed for various rewards.

Scratch Cards

  • Daily scratch cards are available for users.
  • Users can scratch the card to reveal prizes, which may include coins or other rewards.
  • The app offers a limit of 20 scratch cards per day.

Quizzes

  • The app might also feature quizzes where users can answer questions to earn additional coins.

Earning Limitations

  • There is a limit of 20 scratch cards per day.
  • This ensures fair use and prevents abuse of the reward system.

Overall, PlayQuizEarn provides a fun and engaging way to earn small rewards by watching videos and using daily scratch cards.
